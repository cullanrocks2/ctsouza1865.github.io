Accessibility

All images contained on all pages of the website have alternative text that will display in the event that they do not load properly. The functionality of links remains basic in that they all work as intended without need for any javascript. I was receiving contrast errors on all pages due to the background-image and the white text color. To remedy this, I added a background color to my and made them slightly opaque so that the image could still be seen behind them, but still provide enough of a black backdrop to read the text if the image did not load properly. Any discrete links have roll-over color to indicate that there is a link present.
