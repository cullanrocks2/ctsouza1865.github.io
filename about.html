About Me

Now that we have gotten past the basics, let's learn a little bit more about me! I'm currently 23 years old. One of my main interests in computer science stems from my aoration and interest in video games. Over the years, I have developed from a casual gamer to an apspiring pro level competitor.

When I first attended college right out of highschool,I discovered the live-streaming website Twitch, which has grown significantly since I found it. I regularly feel that my experiences and friendships I created through this platform have helped me grow immensely as a person. I myself started to create content and live stream video games. It has become a deep passion of mine that I would love to continue pursuing on the side as I go through life.

Why is that important?

Well, that is why I am here. In the time that I have spent streaming, what I have figured out is that I love sharing the experience of video games with others. I don't necessarily have to do that through live streaming! I could create my own games, or even help in the creation of games that many people already enjoy.
